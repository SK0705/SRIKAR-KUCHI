## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: How long until I see results?
**A:** Typically 4-8 weeks to see initial search visibility. Significant improvements take 3-6 months.

### Q: Do I need to pay for SEO tools?
**A:** No! Free tools from Google are sufficient. Paid tools add convenience but aren't necessary.

### Q: What's more important - keywords or content quality?
**A:** Content quality first, keywords second. Write for users, optimize for search engines.

### Q: How often should I update my portfolio?
**A:** Add new content monthly. Update existing content quarterly.

### Q: Does social media affect SEO?
**A:** Not directly, but high-quality content often gets shared more, which increases visibility.
